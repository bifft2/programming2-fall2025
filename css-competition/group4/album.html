Albums

Weezer's debut album, "The Blue Album," is celebrated for its infectious melodies and raw, emotional lyrics, solidifying the band's place in alternative rock history. Their 2001 release, "The Green Album," marked a more polished sound while retaining their signature quirky charm, featuring hits like "Hash Pipe" and "Island in the Sun." In recent years, albums like "Everything Will Be Alright in the End" showcase a return to their classic style while experimenting with new themes and sounds.
